Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on: 09-02-2025 20:18:05

JAFARKHAN Audio-Loom believes in helping its customers as far as possible and has therefore adopted a liberal cancellation policy.

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made immediately after placing the order. However, cancellation may not be possible if the order has already been processed by vendors/merchants.
  • Cancellations are not accepted for perishable items like flowers, eatables, etc. However, refund/replacement can be made if the customer establishes that the quality of the delivered product is poor.

2. Damaged or Defective Products

  • If you receive a damaged or defective item, please report it to our Customer Service team immediately.
  • The issue must be reported on the same day of receiving the product. The request will be entertained after the merchant verifies the issue.
  • If the product received does not match the description or your expectations, you must contact our customer service within the same day of receiving the product.

3. Manufacturer Warranty

For products that come with a manufacturer's warranty, please contact the manufacturer for any issues.

4. Refund Policy

  • If a refund is approved by JAFARKHAN Audio-Loom, it will be processed within 1-2 days to the end customer.

5. Contact Information

For any queries related to cancellations or refunds, please contact our support team.
